Data products


Coastal cliffs:

RockCoast10Be
A simple backward geometric model developped in MATLAB to explore cosmogenic 10Be concentrations across an active shore platform as a function of cliff retreat and shore platform down-wearing scenarios.

California coastal cliff erosion viewer
Cliff face and top retreat rates along the entire state averaged over regional (50 km) to local (1 km) scales. The rates are based on high-resolution (m) airborne LiDAR and are provided for two monitoring epochs: 1998 to 2010 (Young, 2018) and 2010 to 2016 (Swirad & Young, 2022).

CliffDelineaTool
A MATLAB/Python code to identify coastal cliff base and top positions for ordered cross-shore transects.

Ice:

Ice distribution in Hornsund fjord, Svalbard from Sentinel-1A (2023-2025)
Near-daily binary maps of ice (1) and open water (0) in Hornsund, Svalbard derived from Sentinel-1A Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imagery from July 2023 - August 2025.

Correction to the "Ice distribution in Hornsund fjord, Svalbard from Sentinel-1A/B (2014-2023)" dataset
Three corrected images from 2015-01-18, 2018-12-25 and 2019-02-12.

Ice distribution in Hornsund fjord, Svalbard from Envisat ASAR (2002-2012)
Near-daily binary maps of ice (1) and open water (0) in Hornsund, Svalbard derived from Envisat Advanced Synthetic Aperture Radar (ASAR) imagery from October 2002 - April 2012.

Ice type maps for Hornsund, Svalbard, 1.2012-6.2023 (v1.0)
Near-daily maps of ice types in Hornsund, Svalbard derived from the binary ice/open water maps from January 2012 - June 2023.

Ice distribution in Hornsund fjord, Svalbard from RADARSAT-2 (2012-2016)
Near-daily binary maps of ice (1) and open water (0) in Hornsund, Svalbard derived from RADARSAT-2 Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imagery from January 2012 - March 2016.

Ice distribution in Hornsund fjord, Svalbard from Sentinel-1A/B (2014-2023)
Near-daily binary maps of ice (1) and open water (0) in Hornsund, Svalbard derived from Sentinel-1A/B Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imagery from October 2014 - June 2023.

Waves:

In situ wind wave and water level measurements in Hornsund, Svalbard in 2013-2021
Hourly wind wave spectra and bulk parameters for various locations in Hornsund, Svalbard for July 2013 - February 2021. Input raw pressure sensor measurements at 1 Hz in 1024 s burst and hourly atmospheric pressure, and the Python code are also provided.

Sea pressure and wave monitoring datasets in Hornsund fjord
Pressure sensor data (continuous or bursts) for various locations in Hornsund, Svalbard on-going since July 2013.
