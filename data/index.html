Data products



California coastal cliff erosion viewer
Cliff face and top retreat rates along the entire state averaged over regional (50 km) to local (1 km) scales. The rates are based on high-resolution (m) airborne LiDAR and are provided for two monitoring epochs: 1998 to 2009-2011 (Young, 2018) and 2009-2011 to 2016 (Swirad & Young, In Review).

CliffDelineaTool
A MATLAB/Python code to identify coastal cliff base and top positions for ordered cross-shore transects.
Latest release: Swirad Z.M. 2021. zswirad/CliffDelineaTool: Coastal cliff mapping algorithm with Py version and GMDD modifications (Version v1.2.0). Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.5724975
